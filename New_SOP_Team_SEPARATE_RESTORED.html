SOP – Team Performance Monitoring

  • Red (0–60%) → Critical Risk → Must go into PIP/Firing track.
  • Green (61–90%) → Performing → Stable; sustain performance.
  • Purple (91%+) → High Performer → Recognition + growth opportunities.

Every Day:

  • ASM → SRs: Daily check-in with all SRs (field review, calls, or messages).
  • RSM → ASMs: Daily check-in to ensure ASM reviews are happening.
  • Week 1 – Flag & Escalate — SR/ASM in Red/Yellow flagged immediately; HR notified.
  • Week 2 – PIP Initiation — Individual placed on Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) with targets, trainer and documented sessions.
  • Week 3 – Review — Progress reviewed by ASM + RSM + HR; if partial improvement → additional focused training.
  • Week 4 – 2nd Review — Progress reviewed by ASM + RSM + HR after the 1st Review; if partial improvement → additional focused training and Joint Working with ASM.
  • Week 5 – Final Review & Exit — If still unsatisfactory → Exit executed. If improved → Return to normal monitoring.
