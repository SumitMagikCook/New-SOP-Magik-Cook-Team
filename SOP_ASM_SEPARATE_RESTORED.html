SOP – ASM

  • Purple (91%+) → High Performer → Recognition + Growth Opportunity
  • Green (76–90%) → Performing → Stable; sustain consistency
  • Yellow (60–75%) → Needs Guidance → Coaching + closer review
  • Below 60% (Red) → Attention Required → Immediate support, training & leadership visibility

If any ASM is consistently in Red or Yellow, the RSM will flag immediately.

Every Day: RSM → ASMs: Daily check-in to ensure ASM reviews are happening.

  • Week 1 – Flag & Escalate — ASM in Red/Yellow flagged immediately; HR notified.
  • Week 2 – PIP Initiation — PIP with clear targets, assigned trainer, and documented training sessions.
  • Week 3 – Review — Progress reviewed by RSM + HR; if partial improvement → focused training.
  • Week 4 – 2nd Review — Reviewed by RSM + HR; if partial improvement → additional focused training and Joint Working with RSM.
  • Week 5 – Final Review & Exit — If still unsatisfactory → Exit. If improved → Normal monitoring.
